How long does it take to get a license? On average, 5-30 business days, depending on the type of service.
How much is the state fee for obtaining a license? (AEC) 3,932 tenge. These coefficients may vary depending on the type of service.
Do I need a safe to get a license? A metal cabinet (safe) with a wall thickness of at least 3 mm, firmly attached to the floor or wall, and locked with an internal lock.

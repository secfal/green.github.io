1. Determining the need for a license The list of types of activities subject to licensing is specified in Article 26 of the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On Permits and Notifications". If your activity is subject to licensing, proceed to the next step. 4. For some types of activities, it is necessary to undergo special training and a medical examination before obtaining a license. To obtain a license to possess weapons: According to Article 13 of the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On Weapons", in order to have the right to possess weapons, citizens must: Pass specialized training courses (know the rules for keeping and using weapons). Must obtain a certificate of no objection from narcological and psychiatric dispensaries. Must undergo a medical examination and obtain a certificate of good health. An application is submitted to the internal affairs bodies at their residential address.
2. Collecting the necessary documents To obtain a license, the following is required: For individuals and legal entities: Application (in the prescribed form) Copy of personal identification document For legal entities - registration certificate (BIN) Documents in accordance with special requirements for certain types of activities 5. To obtain a license for security services: In accordance with Article 7 of the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan “On Security Services”, when registering private security organizations: Employees must undergo special training (emergency response, protective equipment, first aid skills). To obtain a permit to use weapons, the above requirements must be met. Driving vehicles and obtaining a driver's license: To obtain a driver's license: You must undergo training at a special driving school. You must undergo a medical examination, including a certificate from a drug and psychiatric dispensary. You must submit documents to the relevant body of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
3. Application An application for a license can be submitted through the "Electronic Government" portal (egov.kz) or directly to the relevant state body. After submitting the application, the established state duty must be paid (Article 554 of the Tax Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan). 6. Must be over 21 years old. Obtain a certificate from psychiatric and narcological dispensaries. Pass a medical examination (vision, nervous system condition, etc.). Pass a special course in weapons handling and obtain a certificate. Have a safe for storing weapons (metal cabinet, wall thickness at least 3 mm). Obtain permission from the internal affairs bodies. 4. Processing time and obtaining a license The processing time for an application may be 5-30 business days, depending on the type of activity. If a license is issued, it will be issued in electronic format (via egov.kz) or in paper form. Additional requirements and features may vary by industry!
